PROJECTS

YOOMP!

Project based on the ATARI game YOOMP! It's made entirely in WebGL without use of any external libraries (apart from the matix math one). In the future there are plans to move it to a better language and massively improve the whole game/renderer.

SYMMETRY

Multiplayer game built in three.js and sockets. To win, player must mirror central board before his opponent will.

DRATEWKA

Remake of ATARI game: "Dratewka the shoemaker", it contains some nice improvements like command autocomplete, map with additional info or AI.
